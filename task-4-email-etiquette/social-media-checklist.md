Do’s
1.	Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments. 
2.	Think before posting — once uploaded, it can stay forever. 
3.	Use privacy settings to protect your personal information. 
4.	Share useful, positive, and meaningful content. 
5.	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news. 
Don’ts
1.	Don’t share personal details like address, phone number, or passwords. 
2.	Don’t post anything offensive, abusive, or harmful. 
3.	Don’t believe or forward unverified news or  rumors. 
4.	Don’t waste excessive time scrolling — manage your screen time. 
5.	Don’t engage in online arguments or cyber  bullying.
