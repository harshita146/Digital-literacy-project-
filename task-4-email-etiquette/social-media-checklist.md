# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments.
- Think before posting — once shared, it can stay online permanently.
- Use privacy settings to protect your personal information.
- Share positive, meaningful, and useful content.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.

##  Don’ts
- Don’t share personal details like your address, phone number, or passwords.
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
- Don’t forward unverified news, rumors, or fake information.
- Don’t spend excessive time scrolling — manage your screen time wisely.
- Don’t engage in online fights, arguments, or cyberbullying.
